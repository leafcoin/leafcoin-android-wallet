Have your Leafcoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Leafcoin Wallet is the first mobile Leafcoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Leafcoin amount in LEAF and mLEAF.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Leafcoin via NFC, QR-codes or Leafcoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Leafcoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Leafcoin balance.

You can find more about Leafcoin at
http://leafco.in

If you want to contribute to Leafcoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/leafcoin/leafcoin-android-wallet

This is a fork of the popular Bitcoin Wallet by Andreas Schildbach and was converted to Leafcoin by me.
Find the original at
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=de.schildbach.wallet
